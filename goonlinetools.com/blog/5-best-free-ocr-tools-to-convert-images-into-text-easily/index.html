5 Best Free OCR Tools to Convert Images into Text Easily

Here are the top 5 free OCR tools that you can make use of:

1. Prepostseo.com

Prepostseo is one of the most famous tools when it comes to optical character recognition. You can upload the image in different forms and all the text is written in that specific file will be extracted and you will be given a normal text file as the result.

While it was just a dream to do so a few years ago, it is a reality now that you can use in your favor.

All you have to do is upload an image from your computer, mobile, or any other place, crop the area out where you don’t want to use the software to search for any text, and let the software do its work.

You can also upload a URL of any image that you want to extract text from, and it will do exactly the same with it. It can be a link from Google, Pinterest, Instagram, or any other social.

If the text in the images is in some other language than English, then you can choose the other languages option in the side menu and convert the whole text into that language instead.

2. Myfreeocr.com

This platform allows you to not only convert images to text one file at a time but converting a whole bulk of pictures at once.

Instead of uploading one image at a time, downloading it, and uploading one again, you can now simply upload a bulk of pictures and you will receive documents after the conversion of those pictures.

This tool is the most reliable as it has multiple built-in engines which makes it easier for you to have a text file without any data loss.

3. ocr2edit.com

This tool helps you differentiate between even the file that you want to create in the end.

While it is flexible with the kind of file you will be uploading on the platform for processing, it also lets you decide what kind of file you will be downloading from it.

Whether you want a normal text file or want a proper word document, you can just choose the option that suits you the best and you will be served accordingly. It is to make sure that you will be able to make use of the file after you convert the image to text right after you get it.

It also helps you choose your files from different platforms and is not just limited to your computer. If the pictures are on a google drive, uploaded on a platform like Pinterest, or in your dropbox, you can just connect that source with the tool to convert image to text. You can use this option if there are multiple pictures and you cannot keep downloading them on your computer to convert the text. If you are a student who is trying to copy the previous work or just trying to get away with an essay pre-written in a book, you are aware of how hard it is to convert images to text manually. This tool provides you with the special functionalities at not just a low price, but absolutely free.

You can use this tool online to convert image to text without having to pay anything and without losing any time. You can just upload the images which contain your data and all of it will be converted to a Word file which you can then download.

You can convert your textbooks, the invoices that you need to make changes to, or old company documents which you want to reuse now.

The cloud system is taking over the world and it is hard to deal with any of the papers in the hard form anymore. You can save time, money, and resources by using this small SEO tool.

5. i2ocr.com

There are three languages involved in using this tool when you want to convert images to text. The first step is to select the language your image has been written in so the text output can be in the same language as well. There is a wide range of languages in which you can convert your image to text for free.

The second step is informing the system whether or not your file a regular file or if it’s a URL. In the third step, you can just simply click on the converted image to text button, and your image will be automatically and magically converted into text.
